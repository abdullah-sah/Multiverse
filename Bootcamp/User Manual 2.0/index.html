My style

What I value

  • Openness/frankess
  • Clarity

What I don't have patience for

  • A lack of consideration for other people

How to best communicate with me

  • I prefer face-to-face communication where possible
  • Discord > Video call > email

How to help me

  • I like to have a clear idea of what my current task or end goal is and what it looks like
  • I always appreciate constructive criticism – be as blunt as you like :)
  • I'd prefer to be nudged in the right direction rather than given a solution where possible
